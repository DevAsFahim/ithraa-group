    About Us

    Ithra Group is the unifying company for both Ithra Al-Khair and Ithra Al-Jood. We are a distinguished and comprehensive Hajj company that provides various services to the guests of God. We are committed to providing an exceptional and comfortable Hajj experience for pilgrims. We offer high quality PVC-A tents to ensure the comfort of pilgrims during Hajj rituals. In addition, our tents are durable and resistant to weather and heat factors, and are spacious in design to accommodate a large number of pilgrims, while providing the necessary privacy for each individual

    Our Services

    Whether you're a first-time pilgrim or a seasoned traveler, our Hajj services offer a comprehensive range of solutions to meet your needs. We provide transportation, accommodation, food services, medical assistance, and religious guidance to make your journey as seamless as possible.
